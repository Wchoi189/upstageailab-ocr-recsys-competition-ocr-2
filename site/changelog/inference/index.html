    Architecture Changes¶

    Before (Monolithic)¶

    InferenceEngine (899 lines)
├── Model loading
├── Preprocessing
├── Inference
├── Postprocessing
└── Preview generation

    After (Modular)¶

    InferenceEngine (298 lines, thin wrapper)
    ↓ delegates to
InferenceOrchestrator (coordinator)
    ├─→ ModelManager
    ├─→ PreprocessingPipeline
    ├─→ PostprocessingPipeline
    └─→ PreviewGenerator
